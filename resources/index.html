Nonaga is a modern abstract tabletop game from Spain.

On your turn you must:

  1. Move one of your marbles in a straight line as far as it can go.
  2. Slide a ring to a position where it touches two other rings.
    1. Your ring must be unobstructed.
    2. It may only pass through gaps of size 2 or larger.
    3. You may not slide the ring your opponent slid last turn.

A player wins when all of their marbles touch at least one of their other marbles.

The current implementation does not support moving rings or win detection.
